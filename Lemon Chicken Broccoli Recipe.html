Lemon Chicken Broccoli Pasta

A delicious fresh lemon chicken pasta dish with broccoli for extra crunch.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • Half broccoli crown
  • Half garlic bulb
  • 50g parmesan
  • zest of 2 lemons
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 350g conchighle pasta shells

Preparation

  1. Dice and fry chicken on a medium heat. Add crushed garlic and lemon zest
  2. Boil pasta for 10 minutes.
  3. When the chicken is cooked through raise heat to high for a minute before turning for another minute. Reduce heat to medium and add broccoli with lemon juice. Cover over with a lid for five minutes.
  4. Place a strainer in a bowl in a sink. empty pasta into strainer. Empty pasta back into the pot and mix with parmesan. Ladel 2 measures of retained pasta water into the mixture, or until it becomes stirrable.
  5. Add fried chicken and broccoli mixture into pot and stir.
  6. Bon Appetit!
